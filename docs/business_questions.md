# Business Questions

## Purpose

This document outlines the primary business questions addressed by the Tableau story and explains why each question matters for retention strategy and decision-making.

## Questions and Business Relevance

- What is the overall churn rate?
Why it matters: Establishes the baseline magnitude of churn and helps quantify retention risk at a portfolio level.

- How many customers churned?
Why it matters: Translates churn percentage into absolute customer impact, supporting prioritization and resource planning.

- Which churn categories are most common?
Why it matters: Identifies broad drivers of churn (for example, competitor, service, pricing), helping teams focus interventions.

- Which churn reasons appear most frequently?
Why it matters: Adds actionable detail for targeted retention actions, messaging, and product or service improvements.

- How does churn vary by payment type?
Why it matters: Highlights potential behavioral and lifecycle differences across payment segments that may affect churn risk.

- How do demographics relate to churn?
Why it matters: Supports audience-level segmentation and helps identify customer groups that may require tailored retention approaches.

- Are customer service calls associated with churn behavior?
Why it matters: Provides directional insight into whether service friction may be linked to churn outcomes.

- Do extra data plan charges show meaningful churn patterns?
Why it matters: Helps evaluate whether usage-related costs may be correlated with churn and whether pricing communication or packaging should be reviewed.
