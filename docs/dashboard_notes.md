# Dashboard Notes

## Objective

This Tableau story is structured to explore churn from multiple business perspectives, combining high-level KPIs with segmented analysis.

## Story Pages

### 1. Churned by Category, Reason and Payment Type
Focus: Overall churn magnitude and distribution by churn category, detailed churn reasons, and payment-type breakdowns.
Use: Supports early identification of dominant churn drivers and potentially affected billing segments.

### 2. Pick Metric by Age Group
Focus: Metric exploration by age group using interactive selection.
Use: Helps compare churn-related behavior across age segments and supports audience-specific interpretation.

### 3. Demographics Analysis
Focus: Relationship between churn and demographic attributes visible in the dashboard.
Use: Assists in identifying potentially higher-risk customer profiles for targeted engagement.

### 4. Customer Service Calls Analysis
Focus: Churn behavior in relation to customer service call patterns.
Use: Provides directional evidence on whether service touchpoints may be associated with churn outcomes.

### 5. Extra Data Plan Average Charges
Focus: Churn patterns relative to extra data plan charges and related cost behavior.
Use: Supports pricing and plan-level interpretation, including possible friction from overage-like charges.

## Interpretation Guidance

The dashboard should be used for exploratory analysis and narrative communication. Findings are best treated as hypotheses until validated against the underlying dataset with deeper statistical methods.
